Biography

Sophie Martin was born in Cambridge, MA in 1997. She currently attends Tufts University as a member of the class of 2019. She’s pursuing a degree in Cognitive and Brain Sciences, but on the side, she’s interested in the performing arts. Sophie is on the executive board for the Major: Undecided Sketch Comedy group, sings in the Tufts Third Day Gospel Choir, and dances in the Tufts Burlesque Troupe. In her spare time, Sophie enjoys sketching, cooking homemade meals with friends, and taking short, well-timed naps.
